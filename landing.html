Common Questions About Renovation in Dubai

What is the best interior fit out company in Dubai?

Centuries Contracting is among Dubai's top-rated interior fit out companies. With 9+ years of experience, 200+ completed projects, and a fully in-house team, we handle everything from design to handover.

How much does home renovation cost in Dubai?

Villa renovations typically range from AED 150,000 to AED 800,000+, while apartment renovations start from AED 50,000. We provide a free, detailed fixed-price quote after a site visit and 3D design.

How long does a villa renovation take in Dubai?

A full villa renovation typically takes 8 to 16 weeks. Our building contractors handle all permits, approvals, and construction with weekly WhatsApp progress photos.

Do you handle Dubai municipality permits?

Yes. We handle all authority approvals including Dubai Municipality permits, DDA approvals, NOCs from developers, and DEWA connections as part of our service.

What areas in Dubai do you cover?

We provide interior fit out and home renovation services across all of Dubai including Arabian Ranches, Dubai Hills, Damac Hills, Jumeirah, JBR, Al Barsha, Palm Jumeirah, and more.

Why choose an in-house team over subcontractors?

Every trade — carpentry, MEP, tiling, painting, landscaping — is on our payroll. One site engineer, one phone number, total accountability. That's why projects finish on time and on budget.
